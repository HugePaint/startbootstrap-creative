Ningbo Yiding Machinery Manufacturing Co., Ltd was established in 2006. We specialized in CNC machining and aluminum die-casting molds, gravity casting mold & low-pressure casting mold. All of our products are exported to the USA, Europe, and Australian.

We have a great engineer team and over 50 CNC machines, including CNC latch, CNC 3-axis & 4-axis vertical machine center, CNC horizontal machine center, CNC hobbing machine, CNC gear shaper, and spline milling, to process the production with ISO quality control. And we also own Patents of invention and design.

YIDING MACHINERY mantra of “SHIFT to a higher level” is exemplified in the daily workings of our company.
